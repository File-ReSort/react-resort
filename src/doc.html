(a) Office of the Comptroller of the Currency LEGAL ORGANIZATION established

There is established in the Department of the Treasury LEGAL ORGANIZATION a bureau to be known as the " Office of the Comptroller of the Currency LEGAL ORGANIZATION " which is charged with assuring the safety and soundness of, and compliance with laws and regulations, fair access to financial services, and fair treatment of customers by, the institutions and other persons subject to its jurisdiction.
(b) Comptroller of the Currency PERSON
(1) In general

The chief officer of the Office of the Comptroller of the Currency LEGAL ORGANIZATION shall be known as the Comptroller of the Currency PERSON . The Comptroller of the Currency PERSON shall perform the duties of the Comptroller of the Currency PERSON under the general direction of the Secretary of the Treasury PERSON . The Secretary of the Treasury PERSON may not delay or prevent the issuance of any rule or the promulgation of any regulation by the Comptroller of the Currency PERSON , and may not intervene in any matter or proceeding before the Comptroller of the Currency PERSON (including agency enforcement actions), unless otherwise specifically provided by law.

The Comptroller of the Currency PERSON is advised by the Secretary of the Treasury PERSON
